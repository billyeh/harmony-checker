You can export a MusicXML file from most popular music editors like Sibelius, Finale or MuseScore.

This site uses Music21 to analyze the music for basic harmony rules as followed in the 17th century, as listed below. It displays the chords as analyzed using VexFlow, along with any errors.

Of course, these rules by no means apply to every piece of music. The philosophy with them is to catch and warn about as many potential errors as possible, allowing the composer to choose whether to ignore them or not.
